Absolute Territory is a mid-range defence type stand that specializes in information control.

Appearance

Absolute Territory is a humanoid Stand that mirrors the slim physique of its user Dante Cupola, with a notable difference in height and gender, as it is smaller and more feminine in design.

Absolute Territory seems to have a white body with black/brown armor parts covering various parts of its body such as its chest, forearms, pelvis, and lower legs. Its overall silhouette resembles a panda.

Its panda inspired color scheme is inspired by Ken Ashcorp's YouTube channel mascot, as the inspiration of the Stand's Name came from one of his songs Absolute Territory, which is also used in famous animated series Pootis Engage also on YouTube.

Personality

Absolute Territory shows no particular personality, usually seen with a stern expression or mimicing its user's. It follows its user's commands without manifesting any will.

Abilities

Absolute Territory is a mid-range Stand, with an absolute range of 10 meters from its user, and a theoretical range of 100 to 120 kilometers from its user. Compared to other similar stands such as Killer Queen or Golden Experience, Absolute Territory is not particularly strong, but it is proven numerous times that it is extremely durable compared to any other stand.

Forbidden Knowledge

Absolute Territory can set any piece of information as Forbidden Information, where upon anyone learning said information after its been set, Absolute Territory and its user will gain significant control over one's body, as well as untouchable by thsose who are affected.

Any piece of information can be set as Forbidden Knowledge, but only one piece of information can be set at a time. This could be a name, a word, a set of numbers, colors of objects, shapes, etc.

This ability can be avoided or may have unintended consequences due to the laws of transformation of information Transformation of Information refers to the idea of transforming information in a way that it is recognizable but fundementally different. Writing about a made up character that is similar in some ways instead of writing a real person, for example. . People who knew the Forbidden Knowledge before it was set and people who forget it are safe and cannot be effected.

Information Sensor

Absolute Territory lets Dante sense the presence of Forbidden Information. Dante can feel when someone or something learns the Forbidden Information, and feel the flow of it. This is a remote ability so it can affect a much larger area than Absolute Territory's absolute range.

Burning Censorship

When someone learns the Forbidden Knowledge, Absolute Territory has the ability to set the victim undable to communicate it. Whenever they try to tell about it, their lips and tongue will burn. If they try to write it, their fingers feel like catching fire.

This ability needs physical contact to be activated, and can persist even if Forbiddne Knowledge is released. But once somone is released from censorship, to activate it again, the victim needs to learn a different piece of Forbidden Knowledge.

King In Yellow

Shortly after Dante's soul touched the stone, in a desperate situation when cornered by his enemies, Aboslute Territory evolved into King In Yellow; A Stand that drives anyone who sees its true form into maddness. For more information, see King In YellowUNDER CONSTRUCTION
